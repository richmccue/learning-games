Eco Sally

A Seashore Restoration Adventure

Run, jump and clean your way down the coast. Collect native seeds and stray trash, dodge invasive crabs and drifting sewage, and bring each shoreline's Biodiversity Index back to full health.

How To Play

Controls

Keyboard: ← → to move, Space to jump.
Touch: use the on-screen arrows and jump button in the bottom corners.

Your Goal

Collect every native seed and piece of trash on the beach to fill the Biodiversity Index all the way up. Once it hits 100%, a restoration beacon lights up near the end of the level, walk into it to finish!

Watch Out For

Invasive crabs patrol the sand, and floating poop bobs and drifts near the water. Touching either costs you a life, and so does falling in the water. You've got three lives per attempt, so tread carefully!

Levels

Three shoreline levels, each trickier than the last: Tide Pool Beach, Mangrove Cove, and Storm Surge Shore. Later levels add moving platforms, faster crabs, and tighter jumps.

Paused

Take a breather. The tide isn't going anywhere.
